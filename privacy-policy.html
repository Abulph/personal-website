Effective Date: 6/30/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how jaymax.co ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website, jaymax.co (the "Site").

By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., by contacting us via email). However, we may automatically collect certain non-personal information through analytics tools, including:

  • IP address (in anonymized form, if available)

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on:
https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. Our Site may use cookies to:

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

We take reasonable measures to protect any information we collect through the Site. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Use the Site at your own risk.

Our Site is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.
