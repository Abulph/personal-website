A few highlights from my portfolio

Farris Builders & Renovators

Home Construction & Remodeling

Farris Builders & Renovators is an Ellettsville, Indiana based home construction and remodeling company. They specialize in transforming homes with expert craftsmanship, offering services that include kitchen and bathroom remodels, custom decks, railings, tiling, painting, doors, windows, and trim carpentry. For their website, Farris Builders wanted a professional, inviting online presence that showcased their portfolio, outlined their wide range of services, and made it simple for potential clients to get in touch for their next project. Visit farrisbuilders.com

Michael Dopp

Artist Portfolio

Michael Dopp is a Los Angeles-based artist born in Bloomington, Indiana. He studied at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, the New York Studio Program, and earned his MFA from UCLA in 2009. His work has been exhibited nationally and internationally. For his website, Michael wanted a minimalist, digital museum-like space where his artwork could speak for itself. Visit michaeldopp.com

Steven E. Lynn, DDS

Dental Practice

Steven E. Lynn, DDS is a family-owned dental practice serving Monroe County and surrounding areas. Dr. Lynn specializes in comprehensive dental care, with a focus on dental implants, including both surgical and restorative procedures in which he holds exclusive certification locally. For his website, Dr. Lynn wanted a clear, trustworthy online presence that reflected his commitment to quality care and professional excellence. Created in collaboration with David Martin Design and Kait Mahl. Visit bloomingtonimplantdentist.com
